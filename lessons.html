Lessons

We have a wide variety of lessons for all levels and budgets. Whether you're just starting out or already have experience, our instructors will cater towards your abilities and help you every step of the way. Even in group classes you will have one on one time and personlized feedback. What are you waiting for? Reserve your spot today!

Group

Our group lessons provide a unique learning environment where you not only learn form the intructor but also from your fellow students. During each lesson there is an oppurtunity to walk around and socialize with your classmates and see how they work around problems you may be having.

Solo

If your looking for a more personlized approach to learning, our individual lessons are made for you. They're tought by professionals that will coach you on everything you need to know. More one on one time means that you will progress faster and be playing your own concerts sooner!

Multi-Week

Our multi-week courses are our most intensive classes. These courses meet multiple times a week and are designed to give you the knowledge you need to progress your skills to professional level. You will complete multiple projects, including a collaboration with another student.

Gigs

What makes us unique is our on-site booking agents that devote their time to get our students live performances. If you purchase a lesson package that includes a gig, we gurantee you will have a concert scheduled by the end of the program. Our main goal is to help you succeed!
